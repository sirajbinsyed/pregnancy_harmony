Pregnancy Journey Timeline

First Trimester

During the first trimester, your body undergoes significant changes as it adapts to pregnancy. This period is crucial for fetal development, so regular prenatal visits are important.

Week 1-12

Second Trimester

In the second trimester, many women feel more energized. It's a time for growth and monitoring the baby's development closely through ultrasounds and other check-ups.

Week 13-26

Third Trimester

As you approach the end of your pregnancy, preparing for labor and delivery is key. Regular prenatal visits during this period help ensure the well-being of both mother and baby.

Week 27-40

Postpartum Care

After delivery, it's important to focus on recovery and adjusting to life with a newborn. Postpartum visits will help monitor your health as well as the baby's growth and development.
